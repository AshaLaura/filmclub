What we are

NOT

Film Club is a monthly IRL gathering in Oakland for people who give a damn about cinema. We watch arthouse and indie films — the kind that actually make you feel something — then take the conversation somewhere with a drink in hand.

No pretension. No algorithm. Just real people, real films, real talk at some of the best venues the Bay has to offer.
